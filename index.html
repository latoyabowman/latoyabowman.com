About Me

Design that connects people to brands

I'm LaToya Bowman, a web designer and developer with a passion for creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. With a background spanning graphic design, web design, content management, project management, and client services, I bring a unique blend of creativity, technical expertise, and business understanding to every project.

I specialise in mobile-first design and development, UI/UX principles, website optimisation, and workflow automation. Whether I'm creating a custom website, improving an existing platform, or streamlining processes, my focus is always on delivering solutions that save time, improve engagement, and support long-term growth.
